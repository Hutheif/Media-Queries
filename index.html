The City

Naivasha is a popular destination in Kenya's Rift Valley, renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and rich biodiversity. The town offers a unique blend of adventure and relaxation, making it an ideal spot for both local and international tourists seeking to explore its natural wonders.

Naivasha is a vibrant town in Nakuru County, Kenya, known for its stunning natural beauty and proximity to Lake Naivasha. It serves as a key hub for agriculture and tourism, attracting visitors with its diverse wildlife and outdoor activities.

FACTS

  • Naivasha is located near Lake Naivasha.
  • The lake is home to over 400 bird species and a significant population of hippos.
